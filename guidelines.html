MONTHLY MELANGE

When: Friday, Dec. 20th

Where: Society Atelier

Please review the swap guidelines in advance.

So, how does this work?

When you arrive, you’ll receive a ticket with a group number - this is your first-round group. Hold on to this - you’ll need to present it to enter your first round.

You will also receive one token for each clothing item you bring - these can be spent as “cash” - one token for one item.

Do...


Bring between 5 and 10 items.

Wash anything that was worn.

Choose lightly used items - would you give this item to your best friend?

Don't...


Bring more than you intend to take.

Bring stained, damaged or smelly items.

Bring non-apparel items (i.e homeware, bedding, etc.).
