Who We Are

The Style Swap Society seeks to blend and build sustainability through community.

We live in a very consumerist society, and it is only natural to want to be stylish and embrace trends that flatter us or enhance our aesthetic appeal. It’s not unhealthy to want a fresh wardrobe on occasion, each year or even each season.

But it is impossible to deny that “fast fashion”, a modern business model of replicating high fashion designs mass produced at low cost and rushed to retail, is destructive to our increasingly fragile climate.

The solution? Clothing swaps! These fun, fashion-forward events allow for us to clear our closets of items we’ve lost interest in or which no longer fit properly without adding to landfills. Our apparel get an expanded lifespan while we get a to explore new or different styles - and all at no cost! It’s truly a win-win.

Style Swap Society is your central hub for all things clothing swap in the Charlottesville region. Come here to find out the who, what, when, where and why swaps are being organized in the area. When we come together as community, the sky is the limit!
